# Page Types

Use the smallest page type that preserves reusable knowledge and source traceability.

## Source Note

Use `wiki/source-notes/` when the page summarizes one raw source.

Template: `templates/source-note.md`

Good for:

- Paper notes.
- Article notes.
- Webpage or WeChat article notes.
- OCR or converted source summaries.

Do not use source notes to synthesize across multiple unrelated sources.

## Concept

Use `wiki/concepts/` when the page explains a reusable idea across one or more sources.

Template: `templates/concept.md`

Good for:

- Definitions.
- Boundaries.
- Related concepts.
- Open questions.

## Comparison

Use `wiki/comparisons/` when the page compares multiple tools, papers, approaches, or claims.

Template: `templates/comparison.md`

Good for:

- Option tradeoffs.
- Capability matrices.
- Source-backed differences.

## Decision

Use `wiki/decisions/` when the page records a technical judgment or selected direction.

Template: `templates/decision.md`

Good for:

- Architecture choices.
- Product direction.
- Accepted constraints.
- Rejected alternatives.

A decision page must cite the source notes or raw sources that support it.
